About Me

Finding balance

Hi, I'm Yeeun — a certified Pilates & Barre instructor. After completing the BASI Pilates Comprehensive program and earning specialized certifications in prenatal/postnatal Pilates and Barre, I offer personalized sessions tailored to every body and every goal.

Before discovering my passion for movement, I built a career in finance — working across securities and asset management. That experience taught me discipline, precision, and a deep appreciation for balance, qualities I now bring to every session.
